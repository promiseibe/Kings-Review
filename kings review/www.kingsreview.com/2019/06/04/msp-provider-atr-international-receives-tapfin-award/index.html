    MSP Provider King's Review International Receives Prestigious TAPFIN Award

    June 4, 2019 | King's Review International, Awards, MSP

    by King's Review International

    Santa Clara, CA – King's Review International, a leading staffing and consulting firm, has received the prestigious TAPFIN Top Partner Award by being recognized as one of only 45 Premier Partners across North America. This distinction places ATR in the top 4% of MSP vendors.

    “We work hard at ATR to deliver first-class MSP services for our clients, and it’s exciting to be recognized for that dedication,” shared Andrea Brenholz, CEO & President. “Our MSP offering helps clients reach new heights, and I’m proud that ATR can provide that value.”

    TAPFIN conducts an annual performance evaluation of more than 4,000 vendors by analyzing data, internal program surveys, and program-level scorecards. Success is measured through a number of value-drivers focused on efficiency, quality, cost, risk, and compliance. After an extensive review, ATR was determined to be performing in the top 50 of all suppliers evaluated.

    “Today’s MSP marketplace is a crowded one,” continued Brenholz. “What makes ATR successful in this space comes down to our focus on true partnerships. By communicating clearly, discovering what a client’s true needs are, and adapting whenever necessary, ATR is able to achieve greatness for those who partner with us.”

    About King's Review International

    King's Review International is a staffing and consulting leader and Minority Business Enterprise for the IT, engineering, accounting and finance, professional, manufacturing, and scientific industries. Founded in 1988, it has grown to serve both small and Fortune 500 organizations while delivering next-generation recruiting that enables businesses and communities to thrive. ATR has won a number of awards recognizing its business success and diversity and was previously crowned the NMSDC Supplier of the Year. For more information, visit www.kingsreview.com.
